Payment Process:

After your order is confirmed with us, payment can be made via EFT. Depending on which bank you use, payment can take up to 2 business days to show in our account. Then as soon as we have your product in hand from ur supplier you will be notified and delivery arranged.

Payments accepted:

We only accept EFT payments as having a credit card facility at our bank has big fees attached to it.

Delivery options:

Collection: If you are in the Pretoria area collection can be arranged at our Faerie Glen office.

Delivery in the East Rand: If you are in the East rand area in Gauteng our team will arrange delivery personally to you.

Delivery Elsewhere: Delivery via Postnet to Postnet as well as delivery via Courier is available at an extra fee of between R100 and R200.
