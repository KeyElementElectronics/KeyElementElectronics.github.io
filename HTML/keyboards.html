How to order:

Since we are a small company and our CEO developed this website himself, our ordering process is a bit janky if we do say so ourselves. When you click the order button below on any of our items you will be redirected to our "Place an order" page where you will find a form to be filled out. Just fill out your details on this form and state the product you are interested in purchasing and we will contact you to confirm stock availability and place your order.
